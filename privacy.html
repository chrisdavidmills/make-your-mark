Privacy policy

We value your privacy.

We only collect the data we need to complete your order, i.e. name, address, phone, and e-mail details. Occasionally it would be used to contact you if there was a query or problem with your order.

With your permission, we would use this data to contact you with any new offers or products we think may be of interest. You may cancel this permission at any time if you wish.

Your payment details, card number, etc. are not held by us, only by Paypal or our card payment handler, Valitor. We will not pass, share or sell your details to any other person or company.
